features

  • smart detection

    automatically detects times in various formats: 3:00 PM, 15:30, 3 PM EST

  • global support

    supports timezone abbreviations, IANA zones, and UTC offsets

  • personal preferences

    set your timezone once and get automatic conversions

commands

/timezone <timezone> - set your personal timezone
/time <message> [timezone] - convert times to your timezone
/mytimezone - show your current timezone and time
/help - show help information and available commands
